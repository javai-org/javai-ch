## Important EU regulatory and policy material

- [European Commission: Artificial intelligence in healthcare](https://health.ec.europa.eu/ehealth-digital-health-and-care/artificial-intelligence-healthcare_en)  
  Useful overview of the EU policy and regulatory landscape for AI in healthcare, including the EU AI Act and health-sector implications.

- [EUR-Lex: Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 — EU AI Act](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/ALL/?uri=CELEX%3A32024R1689)  
  The primary legal text of the EU AI Act. This matters for Swiss pharma whenever AI systems, software, or regulated processes touch the EU market or EU-regulated operations.

- [EMA: Medical devices overview](https://www.ema.europa.eu/en/human-regulatory-overview/medical-devices)  
  Especially important where pharma activity overlaps with AI-enabled software, diagnostics, companion software, or combination-product scenarios.
