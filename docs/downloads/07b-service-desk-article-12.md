# Article 12: Record-keeping

Source: https://ai-act-service-desk.ec.europa.eu/en/ai-act/article-12

## Summary
High-risk AI systems must maintain automatic logging capabilities throughout their lifecycle to record relevant events. These logs should identify potential risks, support post-market monitoring, and track operational data.

## Full Article Text

**Article 12(1):** "High-risk AI systems shall technically allow for the automatic recording of events (logs) over the lifetime of the system."

**Article 12(2):** Logging must enable recording of events relevant to:
- Identifying situations where the system may present a risk or undergo substantial modification
- Facilitating post-market monitoring activities
- Monitoring high-risk system operation

**Article 12(3):** For biometric identification systems (Annex III point 1(a)), logging must provide minimum documentation of:
- Usage periods with specific start and end timestamps
- Reference databases used for data verification
- Input data that produced matches
- Identity of personnel verifying results per Article 14(5)
